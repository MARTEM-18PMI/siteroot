James Bond Edition Citroën 2CV6

Описание:

The 1981 James Bond Edition Citroën 2CV6, abbreviated as Citroën 2CV6 JB, is a custom variant of the Citroën 2CV featured in Forza Horizon 4 as part of the Best of Bond Car Pack.The James Bond Edition 2CV6 was featured in For Your Eyes Only.
